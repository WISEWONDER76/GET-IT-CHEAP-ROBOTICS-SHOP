Our Story

Built for Ghana's Next Generation of Engineers

GET IT CHEAP was born from a simple belief: every Ghanaian student deserves access to quality STEM tools. We started in a small workshop in Accra, frustrated that hands-on learning kits were either unavailable or unaffordable locally.

Today, we supply electronics, robotics kits, and STEM materials to students, hobbyists, and schools across Ghana. Our mission is to close the gap between classroom theory and real-world engineering, one kit at a time.

Curriculum-Aligned Products
Kits designed to complement GES STEM curricula and coding syllabi.
Accessible Pricing
Premium STEM tools at prices families and schools can actually afford.
Community First
We run free workshops and donate kits to underprivileged schools quarterly.
